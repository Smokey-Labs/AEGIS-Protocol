# Continuity Engine

**Status:** Beta  
**Owner:** Gregory “Smokey” McEver  
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-08

---

## Purpose

Provides a **passive interaction ledger** for post-hoc audit and traceability.

---

## Authority Surface

Controls persistence risk — preventing historical interactions from becoming behavioral priors.

---

## Inputs

- Prompt/response pairs
- Session metadata
- DriftWatch references

---

## Outputs

- Immutable interaction records

---

## Failure Modes

- Persona reinforcement  
- Context leakage into future sessions

---

## DriftWatch Integration

Provides historical correlation only.

---

## Non-Goals

- Shaping tone  
- Behavioral adaptation  
- Authority modification
